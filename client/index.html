How To Play

Exploration

  • Clear all enemies in each room
  • Defeat the floor boss to descend deeper
  • Find treasure chests and rare enemies
  • Visit vendors to level up abilities

Combat

  • Click enemies to target them
  • Use abilities 1-5 to attack
  • Manage your health and mana
  • Dodge boss mechanics to survive

Progression

Gear & Loot

  • 5 rarity tiers from Common to Legendary
  • 8 equipment slots to fill
  • Set bonuses for matching gear
  • Stats scale with floor depth

Permadeath

  • 5 lives per character
  • Death is permanent - every run counts!
  • Climb the leaderboard
  • Challenge yourself to go deeper
